Limited Lifetime Warranty

Gadget Medics is happy to offer a limited lifetime warranty to all our customers. We believe that our customers deserve reliable repairs and service they can depend on. By offering a limited lifetime warranty, Gadget Medics hope to prove our quality service and devotion to the communities we service. Please read carefully the terms and conditions under which our limited lifetime warranty applies:

  • Gadget Medics offers a limited lifetime warranty on all parts and labor associated with the repair.
  • This limited warranty can only be applied to the repaired item, and cannot be transferred to a different device.
  • This limited lifetime warranty covers manufacturer-related part defects for parts installed by Gadget Medics technicians. It does not cover any other supplemental or accidental damage.
  • Supplemental or accidental damage can be considered as any damage unrelated to the repair, including, but not limited to, liquid-related or other physical damage.
  • Manufacturer-related defects may include a decline in overall device functionality, touchscreen malfunction, or LCD performance.
  • Any liquid-related or other physical damage which appears after a completed repair will automatically void the warranty.
  • Additionally, the warranty will be void in the event of a software enhancement that can critically affect the functionality of the phone.
  • Gadget Medics reserves the right to resolve disputes concerning this acknowledgement of limited lifetime warranty coverage.
