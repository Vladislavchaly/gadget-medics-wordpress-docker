Who we are?

Gadget Medics represents a team of dedicated professionals whose main goal is to deliver a 100% satisfaction with every repair. Just like any other business, we started with our experience. Many years of hard work in the repair field brought us together. We know our stuff. We know people. But what is the most important, we know how to make every single customer happy.

Using our passion and knowledge in our everyday projects, we are geared up to deliver the most for less. Every single screen is repaired with joy; every shattered iPad is fixed with a smile on the face. We really enjoy what we do and we would like to share that with our beloved customers.

Call us or drop in today, and you'll never go anywhere else. We guarantee that we'll do our best to resolve any issue with your gadgets or we won't deserve any money at all.

Because we know that every business starts from excellent customer service, creating an unforgettable experience is the number one priority for the Gadget Medics crew.
